02 / Trajectory

Two decades, one through-line: build the thing.

2017 → Now

Founder & Managing Director Smaller Agency

Brand & content consultancy serving challenger CPG, wellness, beverage, RTD, and lifestyle brands. End-to-end agency leadership: strategy, creative, production, client services, P&L. Built AI-enabled creative workflows and modular content systems that turned production from a project-based function into a continuous engine across Meta, TikTok, YouTube, and e-commerce.

→
2019 → 2022

Chief Marketing Officer Doseology Sciences

Concurrent fractional CMO for a functional wellness brand. Brand platform, packaging, GTM, retail launch, and DTC growth across Canada and the US.

→
2015 → 2019

VP Marketing GreenSpace Brands

Public CPG portfolio of better-for-you food, beverage, and wellness brands across national grocery and specialty retail. Owned brand strategy, innovation positioning, and integrated marketing across paid, owned, earned, CRM, influencer, and shopper. Partnered with Deloitte on a data-centric marketing approach.

→
2013 → 2015

Director, Brand & Programming Me&Lewis Ideas

Led the SABMiller Canada portfolio (Miller Lite, MGD, Grolsch, Pilsner Urquell, Peroni). Directed the Canadian launch of Miller Lite. Grew the Toronto office 300% in year one and doubled the local roster without adding overhead.

→
2011 → 2013

Director, Development & Strategy Blueband Digital
